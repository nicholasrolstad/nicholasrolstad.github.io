Mineral Potential Analysis

This academic research project analyzed geologic mineral presence indicators using the ArcSDM toolkit in ArcMap in order to create a mineral potential map of sediment-hosted copper in East-Central Idaho. This poster was presented at the 2015 Montana State University Earth Science Colloquium in Bozeman, Montana and the 2016 SME Annual Conference in Phoenix, AZ.
